Heat se Jeet

Temperatures during the last few summers in Karachi have been reaching unprecedented levels and as a result of these heatwaves the number of deaths and illness due to heatstroke and dehydration are tragically on the rise. The most vulnerable to heatwaves are those living in urban slums which lack ventilation, water supply and other basic amenities.

The Heat Se Jeet team aims to provide support to those most at risk from these soaring temperatures. We conduct awareness building sessions at low-income schools and urban slums and organise the distribution of clean drinking water, hydrating foods and even tree planting initiatives. Please join Heat Se Jeet and help those who bear the brunt of the heatwaves. Donations can be made in cash or in kind in the form of hand towels, ORS boxes, fruits and bottled water.
